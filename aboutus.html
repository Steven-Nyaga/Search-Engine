About us!

This is an easy to use website that will assist you in knowing the company you want to work for.

The logo detection system will be a web application. The system will have a database where users and their details e.g. password and contact information will be stored. When a user creates an account he/she can access the service. Once logged into the system the user can search for company vacant positions using categories and tags. After the user finds the job allocation of his choice they can submit their documents such as curriculum vitae. Also, if need be can also do tests given by the organization after applying.
In addition to this, in the case where the user does not have the name of the company he/she is able to write down the moto of the company to search. In case the company’s logo is not registered on the system or the company logo does not exist, the user will receive a message telling him/her that the company does not exist, or the logo simply isn’t registered into the system.
